🎨 Forming Adjectives

📖 Правила:

  • noun + -ful → useful, beautiful
  • noun + -al → magical, musical
  • noun + -ic → optimistic, heroic
  • noun + -ish → selfish, childish
  • noun + -less → helpless, homeless
  • noun + -ous → famous, dangerous
  • verb + -able → reliable, comfortable
  • verb + -ive → active, creative
  • noun + -y → rainy, sunny
1. I was disappointed to learn the children were BEHAVE at the party yesterday.
2. I can't find the receipt. I think I've PLACE it.
3. Don't trust him! He's a HONEST man.
4. I've had a UNDERSTAND with my teacher.
5. Some young people are rude; they often RESPECT their elders.
❌ Forming Negative Adjectives/Nouns/Verbs

📖 Правила:

  • dis- + adjective/noun/verb → disappointed, disorder, dislike
  • mis- + verb/noun → misbehave, misguide, misunderstanding
  • un- + adjective → unhappy, uncomfortable
1. What are the ADVANTAGE of buying from flea markets?
2. The instructions were CLEAR so I made a mistake.
3. She UNDERSTAND the question and gave the wrong answer.
⚡ Forming Verbs

📖 Правила:

  • -ise/-ize → memory → memorise, apology → apologise
  • -en → wide → widen, strong → strengthen
  • -ify → simple → simplify, pure → purify
1. I haven't written his phone number down but I've MEMORY it.
2. Tony was very sorry, so he APOLOGY for breaking the vase.
3. We have to SUMMARY a 3-page text for our English class.
4. Mark has bought an old house which he is going to MODERN.
5. The film CHARACTER the captain as evil.
6. Kim is very kind and she never CRITIC anyone.
🔄 Forming Opposite Adjectives

📖 Правила:

  • il- перед словами с l → legal → illegal
  • im- перед m, p, b → mature → immature, probable → improbable
  • ir- перед r → relevant → irrelevant
  • in- перед другими буквами → dependent → independent
1. The food will be ready in a minute. Don't be so PATIENT.
2. You can't rely on John. He's very RESPONSIBLE.
3. We cannot meet the deadline. It will be POSSIBLE.
4. His decision was LOGICAL; I don't know why he did it.
5. In the UK, it's LEGAL to drive a car if you are under 17.
6. Most of the time, he wears FORMAL clothes to work, not a suit and tie.
📝 Forming Nouns

📖 Правила:

  • -(t)ion → act → action, inform → information
  • -ance/-ence → appear → appearance, depend → dependence
  • -ity → active → activity, able → ability
  • -ness → happy → happiness, kind → kindness
  • -ment → develop → development, achieve → achievement
  • -ship → citizen → citizenship, friend → friendship
1. I would like to thank you for your KIND.
2. For some people, travelling alone is plain MAD.
3. We went on some hair-raising rides at the AMUSE park.
4. You should be proud of your ACHIEVE.
5. He was proud to take part in the hockey CHAMPION.
🔗 Compound Adjectives

📖 Правила:

  • adjective + present/past participle → good-looking, kind-hearted
  • adverb + past participle → well-known, well-educated
  • Используются дефисы для соединения слов
1. old
2. kind
3. open
4. long
5. good
6. well
Пример: I can discuss anything with my parents. They are very open-minded.
